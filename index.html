Temperature, soil moisture, humidity and light intensity were all measured. The frequency range of the temperature and humidity were discovered to be affected by the grow light. Plant growth is influenced by soil moisture and temperature. Compared to LED light source CFL is more efficient and was found to be more suitable for spinach. A system architecture that enables control of the spectral range by choosing the right filter. The Internet of Things was used to create LEDs based on plant requirements developed. Finally, a MATLAB simulation is run to compare pixel values and calculate growth. The growth if observed by leaf length, leaf breadth and stem height. The growth of the leaf is similar in CFL and white LED (1.5cm length,0.8width) whereas 1cm length and 0.5 lead width in violet LED.
